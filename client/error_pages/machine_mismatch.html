License Machine Mismatch

This license is bound to a different machine and cannot be used on this device.

Why is this happening?
Licenses are machine-specific to prevent unauthorized copying. This license was activated on a different computer and cannot be transferred without deactivating it first.
What can I do?
Option 1: Deactivate the license on the original machine
Option 2: Purchase additional machine slots
Option 3: Contact support for assistance
